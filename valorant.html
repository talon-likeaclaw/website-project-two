WE ARE
VALORANT

Blend your style and experience on a global, competitive stage. You have 13 rounds to attack and defend your side using sharp gunplay and tactical abilities. And, with one life per-round, you'll need to think faster than your opponent if you want to survive. Take on foes across Competitive and Unranked modes as well as Team Deathmatch, Escalation, Deathmatch and Spike Rush.

Agents

At the moment Valorant has 22 available agents. As a beginner you only have 5 Agents unlocked to start with: Brimstone, Jett, Phoenix, Sova, and Sage.
By playing the game you can earn Kingdom Credits and unlock the rest of the agents. Each costs 8000KP.
Who to start with?
Valorant has 4 roles: Duelist, Initiator, Sentinel and Controller. For more info consult Agents page.

What's in a round?

Once Agents are selected, you'll jump into a random map and start the game either as a defender or attacker and switch in between of the game. Attacer goal is to plant the and protect spike until it explodes or all enemies are eliminated. Defender goal is to protect sites until the time is up or until all enemies are eliminated.

Guns and Economics

At the beginning of the round you have a classic (default pistol) and a knife. You can purchase any gun you want in the shop. When in doubt, try buying based on a weapon’s type and see how it fits your style, expertise, and your team’s strategy. There are rifles, smg, heavy, shotguns, sidearm and snipers.

Maps

Each map is a different stage to style on your enemies. Flex your creative skills and savvy gunplay for different encounters. For more info consult Maps page.
